A Spirited Origin

Born from the passion to craft India’s most dynamic Alco-Bev company, our journey began with the acquisition of Molson Coors India’s beer business. Since then, we’ve raised the bar with 48 iconic brands, including 8 Millionaire Brands and continue to succeed in blending heritage, craftsmanship and innovation into every bottle.

Ravi S. Deol

Chairman

Ravi S. Deol is a pioneering entrepreneur and Founder-Chairman of Inbrew Beverages, best known for building iconic global consumer businesses, including Barista Coffee. He has shaped Inbrew into a new-age beverage powerhouse through landmark acquisitions such as Molson Coors’ India beer business and Diageo India’s mainstream spirits portfolio. Previously, he transformed Adelie Foods into the U.K.’s leading chilled foods company and led India’s first food-sector SPAC listing on the London Stock Exchange. An alumnus of London Business School, Ravi divides his time between London and Delhi.
