Our Leaders

At Inbrew, our leadership team is at the heart of our vision to transform the beverage industry. Comprising seasoned professionals with diverse experiences and unique insights, our leaders steer the company with a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and exceptional quality. Get to know the individuals whose vision and dedication make Inbrew a dynamic and forward-thinking leader in the market

Ravi S. Deol

Chairman

Ravi S. Deol is a pioneering entrepreneur and Founder-Chairman of Inbrew Beverages, best known for building iconic global consumer businesses, including Barista Coffee. He has shaped Inbrew into a new-age beverage powerhouse through landmark acquisitions such as Molson Coors’ India beer business and Diageo India’s mainstream spirits portfolio. Previously, he transformed Adelie Foods into the U.K.’s leading chilled foods company and led India’s first food-sector SPAC listing on the London Stock Exchange. An alumnus of London Business School, Ravi divides his time between London and Delhi.

Rajnikant Sabnavis

Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer

Rajnikant Sabnavis, Managing Director & CEO of Inbrew Beverages, brings over 30 years of leadership across FMCG, retail, textiles and alco-bev. Since October 2023, he has delivered strong performance, including 25% EBITDA growth and Gross Sales of ₹9,500 crore. Previously, he spent 23 years at Hindustan Unilever in senior leadership roles, revitalised the Radiant (Rin) brand and turned around South Asia Hair Care. He later served as COO at Henkel–Jyothy Labs, CEO of Future Consumer, and Global Head at Grasim. He is an alumnus of NIT Suratkal and SP Jain Institute of Management.

Bhupendra Chhapwale

Chief Financial Officer

Bhupendra Chhapwale is Chief Financial Officer at Inbrew, leading Finance, Legal and IT, with over 27 years of experience across Mondelez, AB InBev, Molson Coors and Cadbury. He played a pivotal role in acquiring and integrating 43 Diageo India brands, delivering strong synergies. Earlier, as CFO at Molson Coors India, he reduced equity infusion by 50% through disciplined working capital management. His revenue initiatives earned Global CEO Mercatus Awards. Bhupendra is a Cost Accountant with an advanced CIMA (U.K.) diploma and has advised global alco-bev companies and start-ups.

Deepak Malhotra

Chief Business Officer, Beer

Deepak Malhotra leads Inbrew’s Beer business, driving strategy, brand equity, expansion and innovation. With nearly three decades in the beverage industry, he has held senior roles at Bira, SABMiller, AB InBev and Beam Global, playing a key role in shaping modern beer culture in India. Known for scaling businesses profitably, building strong distribution and creating categories, Deepak brings deep commercial expertise and a forward-looking approach. At Inbrew, he steers the Beer vertical’s focus on quality, sustainability and long-term growth.

G. Pulla Reddy

Chief Supply Officer & Whole-time Director

G. Pulla Reddy is Chief Supply Officer and Whole-time Director at Inbrew, with 28 years of global experience in supply chain and manufacturing. He oversees integrated Spirits and Beer operations across 35+ manufacturing units, supporting annual sales of over 45 million cases. Previously, he held senior roles at Diageo India, United Spirits and Lexcel Group Nigeria, leading large-scale capacity expansion, modernisation and transformation programmes. A Chemical Engineer from IIT Bombay and SV University, he has completed leadership programmes at IIM Bangalore and Harvard Business School (India chapter).

Manmohan S. Kalsy

Chief Human Resources Officer

Manmohan S. Kalsy is Chief Human Resources Officer at Inbrew Beverages, with over two decades of CHRO and senior HR leadership experience. He has held leadership roles at United Breweries, Medi Assist, Xerox, Triveni Engineering and Go First, and earlier worked with Vodafone, Airtel, PepsiCo, Gillette and DCM. Widely respected for his work in leadership development and organisational transformation, he played a key role during United Breweries’ joint venture with Heineken. An MBA from IPM Meerut, Manmohan is certified by INSEAD, DDI, Hogan, Hay and Gallup.

Susanta Bhattacharya

Vice President, Supply Chain

Susanta Bhattacharya is Vice President & Head of Supply Chain at Inbrew Beverages, overseeing planning, procurement, logistics and contract bottling. With over 20 years of experience at Nestlé, Modi Group and Bharti Del Monte, he brings strong expertise in strategic sourcing and end-to-end supply chain management. At Nestlé, he led South Asia procurement and global innovation partnerships. Earlier, he managed sourcing and agri-retail procurement operations. Susanta holds an MBA from Gauhati University, a degree in Managerial Economics, and CPSM® certification from ISM-USA.

Jaiveer Duggal

Strategy & Future Beverages

Jaiveer S. Dugal leads Strategy & Future Beverages at Inbrew Beverages, driving premium and luxury portfolio development and all inorganic growth initiatives, including mergers, acquisitions and strategic partnerships. He plays a central role in Inbrew’s long-term transformation through premiumisation and category innovation. His experience spans strategy, finance and risk management across GE Capital, American Express, DP World and AB InBev. Known for his analytical rigour and consumer-driven approach, Jaiveer is an alumnus of The Doon School and brings a global perspective to building future-ready beverage businesses.
