Thunderbolt

Originating in the foothills of the Himalayas in 1984, Thunderbolt is the first beer specifically designed for the Indian palate – attributed to the use of Himalayan water in its brewing which gives it its bold and robust flavour. 

Embodying a ‘be more’ proposition, Thunderbolt is all about embracing life's adventures with confidence and boldness – especially when enjoyed with a flavour profile that ensures it leaves a lasting impression.
